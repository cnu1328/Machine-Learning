# Machine Learning: From First Principles to Production

A university-level course combined with industry practice. Designed to transform you from someone who builds ML models with AI assistance into someone who **deeply understands** every line, every equation, and every design decision.

- You know Python and have built ML/DL projects with AI help
- You work in GIS, Computer Vision, and GeoSpatial AI
- You want to understand mathematics, read papers, debug training, and own production systems
- You do **not** want shortcuts

- Attempt exercises before checking solutions
- Pass each module quiz (≥80%)
- Answer mentor gate questions in chat before advancing
- Update `PROGRESS.md` when a module is complete

## Teaching Philosophy

For every concept:

1. Intuition — what is it?
2. Why — why does it exist?
3. Where — industry and research use cases
4. Mathematics — full formulation and derivation
5. Variables — explain every symbol
6. Implementation — code from scratch or line-by-line walkthrough
7. Optimization — how to make it work in practice
8. Mistakes — what goes wrong and why
9. Interviews — how this appears in hiring
10. Production — real-world constraints

## Mentor Interaction

When you finish a module, tell your AI mentor:

> "I completed Module XX. Here are my quiz answers and exercise submissions."

The mentor will ask conceptual questions, review your work, give hints (not direct answers), and unlock the next module.
